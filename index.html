Augmented Reality Experiments

ARCore is an Augmented Reality Platform for building augmented reality apps on Android. Easy to build on supported devices. We used Unity to quickly build a simple app. Installed on Samsung Galaxy S8. We went out in the ground to test ARCore's features. Tracking is good, until you don't move that much fast or run. ARCore will reset all the tracking data, when your fingers cover the camera lens. Device temperature went too high after 15 minutes usage under direct sunlight at 11:00 AM.
